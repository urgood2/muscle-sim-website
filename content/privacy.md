## DATA_COLLECTION_PROTOCOL

This site collects minimal data to function.

### Newsletter Subscription

When you subscribe to the devlog newsletter via Buttondown:
- Your **email address** is stored by [Buttondown](https://buttondown.email)
- Used solely to send devlog updates
- You can unsubscribe anytime via the link in each email
- Buttondown's privacy policy: [buttondown.email/legal/privacy](https://buttondown.email/legal/privacy)

### Contact Form

When you use the contact form:
- Your **name, email, and message** are sent to me via [FormSubmit](https://formsubmit.co)
- Used only to respond to your inquiry
- Not stored in any database or shared with third parties

### Analytics

This site does not use cookies or tracking analytics.

### Third-Party Services

- **Ko-fi**: If you choose to support via Ko-fi, their [privacy policy](https://ko-fi.com/home/privacy) applies
- **Disqus**: Comments are handled by Disqus; their [privacy policy](https://disqus.com/privacy-policy/) applies
- **GitHub Pages**: This site is hosted on GitHub Pages; their [privacy statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement) applies

### Your Rights

You can request deletion of your data by contacting me through the [About](/about/) page contact form or via Discord.
